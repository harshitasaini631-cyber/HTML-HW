Space Docking Experiment (SpaDeX) by ISRO

India became the 4th country to successfully carry out space docking between two small satellites, a major milestone for satellite servicing and future space station developments.

India’s Quantum Computer “Indus” (25‑qubit)

The startup QpiAI developed one of India's first full‑stack quantum computers with 25 qubits under the National Quantum Mission, marking progress in quantum computing capabilities.

AIRAWAT Supercomputer

C‑DAC’s AIRAWAT is India’s first, fastest, and largest AI supercomputer (as of its installation), with a capacity of ~13,170 teraflops. It has been ranked in the Top 500 global supercomputers.
